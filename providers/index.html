Each treatment, alone & combined.

When patients are on overlapping preventives, Ember tracks each medication’s contribution and the combined effect. For example, Botox alone might show a 40% improvement over baseline. A CGRP mAb alone, 40%. Together, they give a patient 78% reduction.

Ember reports the numbers cleanly, so the case for continuing therapy is easier to make. The continuation decisions are yours. The data is presented so you have everything you need to make the best clinical calls, or to advocate for your patients in prior authorizations and appeals.

Safety flags built in.

Ember tracks acute medication use against published thresholds that indicate medication adaptation headache risk, and flags patients as they approach the limits. The approach is non-judgmental. Patients are never shamed for taking their medications.

Reports what they logged. Nothing more.

Ember structures data; it doesn’t interpret it. The patient’s logs stay on their phone. We never see them. The clinical decisions remain yours.
