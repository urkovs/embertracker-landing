How it works

A check-in, every evening.

We get it – the last thing you want to do during an attack is look at your phone.

Ember waits until the evening. Tell it how today was. Add symptoms or meds if you want. That’s the whole check-in. Easy to do from bed, with the lights low.

01

Any head pain today?

Four options: none, mild, moderate, severe. Choose the one that fits.

02

Symptoms + Meds?

Pick any symptoms you noticed or medications you took. Skip what does not apply.

03

Ember takes it from there

Your check-in becomes part of your calendar and your report.
