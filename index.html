Why Ember

What no other tracker does.

01

Automatic classification

Headache day or migraine day? You log your symptoms. Ember applies the same clinical criteria your headache provider uses and classifies every day automatically. No guesswork.

02

Reports your provider can use

Frequency, severity, disability scores, treatment response, and medication patterns. Formatted for prior authorization renewals on Botox, Ajovy, Qulipta, Emgality, and other preventives. Copy the chart note and send it to your provider.

03

When 50% isn't the whole story

Insurance wants a 50% reduction in frequency. But you might be dramatically better in severity, function, and disability without hitting that number. Ember captures all of it so your improvement is documented, not just counted.

04

Dark and quiet

Pure black background. Muted earth tones. No bright screens, no visual clutter. Designed for people who track their migraines during migraines.

How it works

Under a minute, every day.

1

Tap your severity

No pain, mild, moderate, or severe. One tap. If it's a clear day, you're done in five seconds.

2

Select your symptoms and meds

Your personal symptom and medication chips appear automatically. Tap what applies. Ember classifies the day in real time.

3

Ember does the rest

Headache day or migraine day. Frequency trends, severity averages, treatment response, rebound risk. All calculated, all exportable, all formatted for your next provider visit.

Your data stays on your device.

All health data is stored locally on your phone. Nothing is sent to servers, third parties, or the cloud. You control what you share and with whom.
