What we ask of beta testers

  • Tell us everything. There will be bugs. Report them all. Feedback, issues, ideas, questions, gripes, half-formed thoughts — everything is welcome. We read and respond to all of it.

  • Your data comes with you. Everything you log during the beta stays with you when you move over to the official App Store version. Nothing gets left behind.

We will use this only to send you a TestFlight link and the occasional beta update. Your address stays with us.

Giving feedback

Screenshot anything, then tap Share Beta Feedback…

Fastest way to flag a bug or suggestion. Take a screenshot anywhere in Ember, tap the preview, and choose Share Beta Feedback… from the iOS menu. The screenshot comes to us with your note attached.

Exploring sample data

Tap Ember v1.0 five times to load sample data.

Want to see what Ember looks like with data in it? Open your Profile, scroll to the bottom, and quickly tap the version text five times. Ember loads 180 days of sample data so you can explore reports and patterns right away.

To clear it and start fresh, tap the version text seven times — or use Delete my account and all data from Profile.
